05 January 2022

Security Analyst

Kyiv, Odesa, Rivne, Zhytomyr, Zaporizhia, Tbilisi (Georgia), remote

Required skills:

  • 5+ of experience in security or technical support roles;
  • Analytical and problem-solving skills to independently identify and assess risks, threats, patterns, and trends, then provide that information or choice to others;
  • Strong interpersonal skills, ability to work with non-technical users to gather precise incident details;
  • Strong writing skills and an ability to describe complex technical issues with depth and structure. Must have the ability to organize information to convey timing, cause, effect, and desired outcome;
  • Previous experience supporting computer networks, operating systems, software, and hardware;
  • Understanding of common email phishing and other exploitation techniques. Ability to rate emails as malicious/clean and take necessary steps;
  • Familiarity with common end-user attack vectors for credential theft, malware delivery, wire fraud, etc;
  • Understanding of Active Directory permissions;
  • Familiarity with common diagnostic tools like nmap, procmon, bind, etc;
  • Experience searching with Splunk and other security tools;
  • Familiarity with scripting in Powershell and/or Python, desired;
  • Working knowledge of security technologies such as firewalls, anti-malware agents, and security logging/tracing.

Responsibilities:

  • Analyze user-reported security matters: suspicious emails, unusual logon activity, etc. Communicate with users to gather details and/or explain the threat;
  • Analyze system-detected security alerts. Research and resolve or escalate;
  • Analyze system-detected vulnerabilities. Verify the risk and route to the responsible party;
  • Maintain master data in monitoring systems to ensure alerts have full context.

We offer:

  • To work with company which is financially stable and reliable, we dont have staff cut or salary cut, on the contrary- our company is growing and developing. As our main customers are American insurance companies, it allows us to feel safe and protected on financial side;
  • Health insurance;
  • English classes (online) and speaking-club with native speakers;
  • Possibility of remote work;
  • Competitive salary;
  • Collaborative friendly team environment.

About project:

Virtuace on behalf of our client — American insurance company is looking for a Security Analyst to join our team.
Client is a global specialty provider of property and casualty insurance and reinsurance that has more than $110 billion USD in total assets along with $17 billion USD in net assets.
